# Eliminating Legacy Document Burden with Localized AI Extraction

In heavily regulated sectors—such as civil engineering, infrastructure, and institutional procurement—decision-making is tied to massive documentation. Tender specification documents, liability addendums, and compliance clauses often span thousands of pages.

**Duneborn Scribe** is engineered specifically to eliminate this document burden through high-precision localized AI extraction.

---

## The Challenge of Unstructured PDF Extraction

Off-the-shelf OCR engines simply convert pixels into raw text. They lack structural comprehension:

- They fail on multi-column tables.
- They miss key indemnity caps buried in dense legalese.
- They cannot link related clauses across separate annexures.

---

## The Scribe Solution

Duneborn Scribe utilizes custom vision-language models fine-tuned on structural blueprints and legal contracts.

```text
Input Document: tender_spec_civil_v4.pdf (14.2 MB)
Processing Mode: Secure Local Extraction

[1/3] Parsing Layout Schematics ... DONE
[2/3] Extracting Indemnity Caps & Standard Clauses ... DONE
[3/3] Cross-referencing ISO-27001 Standards ... VERIFIED
```

### Key Highlights

- **Private On-Premises Option**: Run models locally inside customer data centers to meet strict NDA constraints.
- **Instant Structured JSON**: Turn multi-page PDFs into actionable JSON parameters for instant workflow ingestion.
- **Accuracy Guarantee**: Zero hallucination through deterministic grounding techniques.
